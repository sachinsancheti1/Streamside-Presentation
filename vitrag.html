Who we are

Streamside is located in the scenic Ketti valley, a half way drive from Coonoor to Ooty. The location boasts of spectacular views of the valley & breath-taking mountain tops of the Nilgiris hill range.


Among the 13 villas at Streamside, 6 are under construction with 2 fully equipped for families ready to move in. Each villa is designed to house both nuclear and joint families in a unique and salubrious environment.


The homes are Vaasthu compliant and have been created to allow abundant natural light and fresh air. These Victorian- styled homes have spacious rooms with a judicious blend of wood panelled roofs, parquet flooring for bedrooms, and a ready to use kitchen.
