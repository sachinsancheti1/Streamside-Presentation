Streamside

Streamside is located in the scenic Ketti valley, a half way drive from Coonoor to Ooty. The location boasts of spectacular views of the valley & breathtaking mountain tops of the Nilgiris hill range.

Streamside Homes

At Streamside, we are coming up with 14 homes that will fill the 2 acre property with life. The choice of homes are spread from 1,200 sq.ft to 2,450 sq.ft, catering to both small and large families to stay together in this unique salubrious environment.

Central Park

Multipurpose Court

A 4,000 sq.ft. court which will cater to games like basketball and badminton. Flood lighting at night makes this place a favourite passtime for residents after dusk

Central Lawn

Beside the court is a 8,000 sq.ft. lawn with gazebos and sitting areas serving as your casual reading and as a party zone

Walking & Cycling track

Bringing wellness to your doorstep, we have a walking track as well as a cycling track within the premises just beside the court and lawn area

The Finest

Most balconies of each home look out into the central courtyard while the rest look into the private gardens and the mountains surrounding Streamside. Gushing water, chirping birds and gentle winds fill the air with life in the lap of the Nilgiris.

Stream @ Streamside

At Streamside, you will find nature at its finest, with the stream meandering below into the meadows, untouched forests, mountains and into the horizon

Nilgiri Mountain Railway

The iconic heritage Nilgiri Mountain Railway passes along the periphery of the property. The unique charm of this train is feast to the eyes of the on-looker.
